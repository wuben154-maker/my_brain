## Selecting a Pairing

**By app type:**
- Dating/social → Tech Startup or Geometric Modern
- E-commerce → E-commerce Clean (Rubik + Nunito Sans)
- SaaS/dashboard → Minimal Swiss or Friendly SaaS
- Health/wellness → Wellness Calm or Medical Clean
- Gaming → Gaming Bold or Crypto/Web3
- Luxury → Classic Elegant or Luxury Minimalist

**By mood:**
- Professional/trust → Financial Trust (IBM Plex Sans)
- Playful/fun → Playful Creative or Kids/Education
- Bold/impact → Bold Statement or Gen Z Brutal
- Calm/serene → Wellness Calm or Soft Rounded
- Techy/modern → Developer Mono or Tech Startup
